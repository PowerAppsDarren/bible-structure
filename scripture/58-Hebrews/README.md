# Hebrews

## Overview

A masterful argument for the superiority of Christ over the Old Testament system -- superior to angels, Moses, the priesthood, and the old covenant.

## Author

Unknown (traditionally attributed to Paul; possibly Apollos, Barnabas, or Luke)

## Date Written

c. AD 64-68

## Chapters

13

## Key Themes

- Supremacy of Christ
- Faith
- The new covenant
- Perseverance
- Rest in God
