# 1 Samuel

## Overview

The transition from the judges to the monarchy, covering the lives of Samuel, Saul, and the early years of David.

## Author

Samuel, Nathan, and Gad

## Date Written

c. 1050–900 BC

## Chapters

31

## Key Themes

- Kingship
- Obedience
- God's sovereignty
- Anointing
- Faith and courage
