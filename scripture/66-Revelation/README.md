# Revelation

## Overview

An apocalyptic vision given to John on the island of Patmos, revealing the ultimate triumph of Christ, the defeat of evil, and the new heaven and new earth.

## Author

John (the apostle)

## Date Written

c. AD 94-96

## Chapters

22

## Key Themes

- The return of Christ
- Judgment
- Worship
- Victory over evil
- The new creation
- God's sovereignty
