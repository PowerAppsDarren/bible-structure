# Jeremiah

## Overview

The prophet Jeremiah warns Judah of impending judgment, calls for repentance, and foretells a new covenant.

## Author

Jeremiah (with Baruch as scribe)

## Date Written

c. 627–580 BC

## Chapters

52

## Key Themes

- Judgment
- Repentance
- The new covenant
- Faithfulness
- Suffering of the prophet
