# Psalms

## Overview

A collection of 150 songs, prayers, and poems expressing the full range of human experience before God.

## Author

David, Asaph, Sons of Korah, Solomon, Moses, and others

## Date Written

c. 1440–430 BC (spanning roughly 1,000 years)

## Chapters

150

## Key Themes

- Worship
- Lament
- Praise
- Trust
- The Messiah
- God's faithfulness
