# Job

## Overview

A poetic exploration of suffering, faith, and divine sovereignty, following a righteous man tested by extraordinary loss.

## Author

Unknown (possibly Job, Moses, or Solomon)

## Date Written

Unknown (patriarchal period setting; written c. 2000–500 BC)

42

## Key Themes

- Suffering
- Sovereignty of God
- Faith under trial
- Wisdom
- Divine justice
