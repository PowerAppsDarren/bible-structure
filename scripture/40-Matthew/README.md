# Matthew

## Overview

The Gospel written primarily for a Jewish audience, presenting Jesus as the promised Messiah and King through His teachings, miracles, death, and resurrection.

## Author

Matthew (Levi)

## Date Written

c. AD 55-70

## Chapters

28

## Key Themes

- Jesus as Messiah
- The kingdom of heaven
- Fulfillment of prophecy
- Discipleship
- The Great Commission
